NHRC DB9 connector
pin 1 - test button                                     DCD     RED
pin 2 - audio left (connected to audio in left)         
pin 3 - audio right (connected to audio in right)
pin 4 - red - drives red LED                            DTR     WHITE
pin 5 - tx audio monitor
pin 6 - green - record signal                           DSR     GREEN
pin 7 - yellow - drives green LED                       RTS     YELLOW
pin 8 - blue - record signal                            CTS     BLUE
pin 9 - tx audio monitor

lower case: repeater wiring
upper case: USB to serial cable colour.
